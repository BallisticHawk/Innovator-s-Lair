My Innovative Projects Showcase

Below are the projects I have done over the years in search for new ideas and innovative solutions. P.S. = Its just a preview showcase of the main idea, more pictures are in the embedded links.

H-2-gO (First Project)

This project started when I was 14 years old and I have 1 goal in mind. To create a bottle that can filter water while remain compact for military or camper use but as you can see, with time it soon evolved into something else. A bottle that has charging, heating and storage capabilities all in a single compact format.

The Levitator (Second Project, Futuristic Car)

This project started when I was 15 and during that time I have envision a dream where cars could go anywhere, ranging from the skies, down to the depths of sea. The core ideas put in this car was mainly sustainable palm oil fuel, electrolysis for oxygen generation, solar panels for electric generation and jet engines for levitation.

Trash-Sorter (Third Project)

This invention was made during the time where recycling became very popular and people were discussing about litering, so it started from a portable compress bin that compresses your trash at a pocket of your backpack, afterwards I began to incline to the recycling side of things and how to sort rubbish better. so I thought what if we could make this sorting process easier. This is where the trash sorter is born, a fully automated trashcan that sorts ur rubbish and can be cleared by compartment with self cleaning function.

Palm Sorter (Fourth Project)

In conjunciton with a competition I participated in, we were told to create a product that is useful to our economy. So it started from palm oil as Malaysia is one of the biggest palm oil distributor. My thought process was how do I make the palm oil process easier in order to save time and cost. After much research, I realise that the pre-stage sorting process took a bit long, so I created a trolly that plucks the palms out of the branches and wash them on the fly during the harvesting process so that they can be pressurised steam clean immediately after the harvesting process.

Vending-4-Mending(Fifth Project)

Vending-4-Mending stands for vending in order to mend stomachs. This idea came to us when we had a mission in sight, to help college students or busy workers get healthy food while making it as quick as instant noodles. During our research we found out that college students and busy workers often eat instant noodles or other unhealthy alternatives in order to catch up on work. This is because ordering food on the fly sometimes takes too long. Our solution? Create a vending a machine that vends out healthy food and requires constant mantainance as the foods are always fresh.
